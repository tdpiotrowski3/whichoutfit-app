How It Works

From empty app to
your smartest wardrobe.

WhichOutfit turns the clothes you already own into a personal styling assistant. Here's exactly how it works — start to finish, in under ten minutes.

The big picture

Styled in three steps

Everything else is detail. At its heart, the app does three things.

1

Add your clothes

Take photos, paste product links, or upload a receipt. The AI auto-tags everything — your closet builds itself.

2

Ask your stylist

"What do I wear tonight?", "Rate this outfit", "Pack for Mykonos." The app knows your wardrobe and your taste.

3

Dress with confidence

Get dressed faster, look better, and stop buying things you already own.

Step 1 · Build your closet

Four ways to add.
None of them a form.

Pick whichever is fastest. Every method lets the AI do the typing — you just review and save.

📸

Snap a photo

Tap + → Add Photo and shoot any garment. The item lands in your grid instantly with a "Tagging…" badge while AI fills in the category, colour, fabric, brand, and style tags in the background.

AI Vision
🔗

Paste a product link

Tap + → Import from Link and drop any product URL. The app loads the page from your device, pulls the image and details, and pre-fills every field for you.

Most retailers
🧾

Upload a receipt

Tap + → Add from Receipt and upload an order screenshot. The AI reads every clothing line item — skipping shipping and tax — so you can bulk-add a whole haul at once.

Bulk add
✍️

Add it by hand

Prefer full control? Enter any item manually. Every field the AI fills — name, colour, fabric, tags, size, brand — is editable at any time by tapping the item.

Always editable

Tagging runs in the background, so you never wait. If it ever fails, a yellow Retry badge appears on the item — tap it to try again.

Step 2 · Ask your stylist

One bar. Ask it anything.

The AI Concierge on your Closet home screen understands plain language and opens the right tool — pre-filled with whatever it can work out from your question.

👗

"What do I wear tonight?"

Get outfit ideas built entirely from pieces you already own, tuned to the occasion, the weather, and your style profile.

⭐

"Rate this outfit"

Attach a photo and get a score out of 10 with specific, actionable feedback. Choose Gentle, Balanced, or Honest feedback in settings.

🧳

"Pack for a weekend in Rome"

The Trip Planner builds a capsule from your real wardrobe for your dates and destination — no generic internet lists.

🔍

"Find something like this"

Snap inspiration and the app surfaces the closest matches in your own closet, so you can recreate a look with what you have.

Every request is answered with your full closet, style profile, and recent worn log as context — that's why the suggestions actually fit you.

Step 3 · It gets to know you

The more you use it,
the better it dresses you.

Nothing to configure — the app keeps learning your taste from the way you actually use it.

🎯

Style profile

A quick two-minute quiz captures your vibe, favourite occasions, and sizing. Update it anytime — every suggestion is personalised to it.

📅

Worn log

Log what you wear and the app learns your real habits. Your last entries feed every AI answer so nothing feels generic.

☁️

Backup & sync

Premium keeps your whole closet safely backed up and in sync across devices, so it's always there when you switch phones.

Your closet stays private, and your photos are never used to train AI models. See the Privacy Policy for the details.
