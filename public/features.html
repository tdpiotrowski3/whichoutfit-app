Build Your Closet

Add everything you own.
The app does the typing.

Four ways to build your closet — none of them require manually filling out a form.

📸

Snap & Auto-Tag

Take a photo of any garment and the app fills in the category, colour, fabric, brand, and style tags — instantly, in the background, while you keep browsing your closet. Every field stays editable, and every tag becomes searchable: type "green linen" or "date night" and find it.

No typing
🔗

Import from a Link

Paste any product link — ASOS, Zara, H&M, Net-a-Porter, anywhere. The app fetches the page directly from your device, pulls the image and details, and pre-fills every field. Review the result, tap Save. Done.

Most retailers
🧾

Receipt Import

Upload a screenshot of any order confirmation. The app reads every clothing line item — skipping shipping, taxes, and gift cards — and shows you a review list. Select what you want, tap Add, and a whole haul lands in your closet at once.

Whole orders in one go
🪄

Photo Cleanup

Photo Studio turns quick snaps into catalog-worthy shots: backgrounds removed, garments crisply framed — even shoes get a clean side-profile treatment. Your closet grid stays consistent and clutter-free, whatever was behind the camera.

Catalog-clean grid

Organizing is just as fast: multi-select to retag, set a brand or price on several pieces at once, or clean up in bulk. Add extra angles to any item — a side or back view — and pick your main shot.

Your Personal Stylist

A stylist that knows
your wardrobe cold.

Every suggestion is built from your actual closet and style profile — not generic internet advice.

✨

Rate My Outfit

Score a look before you leave. Snap what you're wearing and get a 0–100 score with a clear verdict: what's working, what isn't, and swaps or upgrades pulled from your own closet. You choose who's rating you — Gentle, Balanced, or Honest — from encouraging coach to no-mercy fashion editor.

Scored out of 100
🎭

Dress for an Event

"Dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant." "Casual Friday." "A summer wedding in Tuscany." Name the occasion and the app builds a specific outfit from your wardrobe, with alternatives — and a shop section only for genuine gaps.

Any occasion
👗

See It On

Preview any outfit before you put it on — on a studio mannequin, on a model, or on you. When a look works, share it as a branded try-on image.

Virtual try-on
🔍

Scan an Item

About to buy? Point your camera at any piece — in a shop, on a friend — and the app checks your closet for duplicates, shows what it pairs with, and suggests how it would style with what you already own.

Spot duplicates
📷

Inspo Snap

Screenshot an outfit from Instagram, a magazine, anywhere. The app decomposes it into individual pieces, matches each one to items you already own, and shows how close you can get — with shoppable links for the rest.

Recreate any look
🗳️

Ask Friends

Torn between two fits? Share a "Which Fit?" poll and let friends vote yes or no, or pick between options — straight from a link, no app required to vote.

No app needed to vote

Or just type what you need — "what do I wear to a rooftop dinner?" — and the app opens the right tool, pre-filled.

before you open your closet.

Weekly Planner

Outfit ideas for your whole week, built around live weather and your calendar. A work presentation, a date night, a friend's birthday brunch — the planner sees what's coming and dresses you for it. Calendar access is read-only, always.

Weather + calendar aware

Trips & Packing

Enter a destination and what's on the schedule — dinner, beach, hiking, a wedding, the flight home. The app builds a capsule wardrobe and packing list from pieces you already own, sized to the forecast. Refine it naturally: "make day 2 more casual."

Capsule + packing list
📲

Today's Fit

Wake up to an outfit picked for the day's weather and plans — on your home-screen widget, in your morning notification, and in the app, all in sync. One tap to log what you actually wore.

Widget + notification

Know Your Style

The more you use it,
the better it gets.

Your style profile, worn history, and sizing all shape what the app suggests — every suggestion gets sharper over time.

🎨

Style Profile & Quiz

A quick quiz builds your taste signal: the vibes that resonate, style icons whose wardrobes you'd raid, the occasions you actually dress for, and how bold you like to go. Every suggestion is personalised to it. Retake it anytime.

Suggestions that fit your taste
📅

Worn Log

Snap today's outfit and the app spots which pieces you wore — new pieces can join your closet in a tap. Over time it learns what you actually reach for, what sits unworn, and which combinations you keep coming back to.

Learns your real habits
🔖

Saved Looks

Bookmark the winners. Any outfit — scored, planned, or logged — can be saved and pulled back up the next time you need a sure thing.

Your greatest hits
📏

My Sizes

Sizing per category — tops, bottoms, full body, shoes — in US, EU, UK, or IT, with room for multiple sizes because brands never agree. Sizes inform fit suggestions only; they never gate or filter what you're shown.

Fit, never limits

Style has no gender here. Suggestions never assume who you are — sizes inform fit, never limits.
